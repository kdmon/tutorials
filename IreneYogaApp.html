About the App

This App gives a list of yoga postures with the English and Sanskrit names.Each posture has an image and the benefits that can be derived by regular practice. There is an audio button giving instructions on how to practice the posture.

Who would find it useful

This app is designed for yoga practitioners who want a quick translation of terms from English into French which would be useful during a class or private study.
